Jami: A New Era for Creator Tipping

Creators everywhere are looking for better ways to be supported by their communities — especially independent writers, artists, podcasters, educators, and performers who don’t rely on big platform algorithms to be discovered.

Enter Jami, a tipping and monetization platform that’s gaining traction — particularly across Africa and in global creator communities — by giving creators the tools to get paid directly by their fans.

What Is Jami?
Jami is a unified monetization platform designed to help creators showcase their work, connect with supporters, and earn money directly — without complex subscriptions, delayed payouts, or restrictive rules.

With Jami, creators can share their personalized page where fans can tip, buy products, or even subscribe for exclusive content.

Why Creators Love Jami:
- Instant payouts to your bank account or wallet.
- Local and global payment support: M-Pesa, Visa, Mastercard, and even crypto.
- No fees: Jami lets creators keep most of their earnings.
- Easy to set up: Create your page in minutes, no upfront costs.
